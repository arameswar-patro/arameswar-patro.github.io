Selected Projects

Word Maps on Groups [cite: 33]

Advisor: Dr. Tushar Kanta Naik, NISER | Summer 2025 [cite: 34, 35, 37]

Investigated word maps across finite, profinite, and algebraic groups[cite: 36]. Examined Waring-type problems, power maps \( (x \mapsto x^M) \), and probabilistic aspects related to character theory[cite: 38].

Geometric Invariant Theory [cite: 21]

Advisor: Dr. Mahmut Levent Doğan, DRP Turkiye | 2025-2026 [cite: 22, 27, 28]

Studied Hilbert's Finiteness Theorem, Matsushima's Criterion, Hilbert-Mumford Theorem, and the Kempf-Ness Theorem[cite: 32].

Groups and Geometry [cite: 39]

Advisor: Dr. Krishnendu Gongopadhyay, IISER Mohali | Summer 2024 [cite: 40, 41, 42]

Supported by the Indian Academy of Sciences Summer Research Fellowship[cite: 43]. Studied isometries of the Euclidean plane, Frieze and Discontinuous groups, and the structure of triangle groups[cite: 45]. Explored regular tilings and introductory hyperbolic geometry[cite: 46].

Combinatorial Aspects of Coxeter Groups [cite: 47]

Advisor: Dr. Tushar Kanta Naik, NISER | Summer 2023 [cite: 48, 49, 50]

Explored reduced expressions, length functions, the strong exchange condition, and Bruhat order[cite: 51]. Constructed geometric realizations of Coxeter groups using root systems[cite: 52].
